# Strengths by Use Case

# Strengths by Use Case

No single model wins everything. Pick based on what you're actually doing.

## 💻 Coding

**Best: Claude Opus 4.8**

Claude dominates real-world coding. It scores 88.6% on SWE-bench Verified (May 2026) and powers the leading dev tools — Cursor, Windsurf, and Claude Code. Even where Grok 4 and GPT-5.4 match on raw SWE-bench numbers (~74–75%), developers report Claude's code quality and explanations are superior in practice.

- Claude Opus 4.8: 88.6% SWE-bench ✅
- Grok 4: 75% SWE-bench
- GPT-5.4: 74.9% SWE-bench

## 🧠 Reasoning & Science

**Best: Gemini 3.1 Pro**

Gemini leads every published reasoning benchmark as of May 2026. For graduate-level science, mathematical proofs, and novel logic problems it's the clear choice. Claude Opus 4.7 is a close second.

- Gemini 3.1 Pro: 94.3% GPQA Diamond, 77.1% ARC-AGI-2 ✅
- Claude Opus 4.7: ~91% GPQA Diamond
- Grok 4: Leads Humanity's Last Exam (50.7%) — best at absolute frontier knowledge

## ✍️ Writing & Long-Form Content

**Best: Claude**

Claude produces the most natural prose among frontier models and can output up to 128K tokens in a single pass — essential for long documents, reports, and books. GPT-5.5 is a close second with broad stylistic range. Gemini underperforms on long structured writing.

- Claude: Best prose quality, 128K output ceiling ✅
- GPT-5.5: Strong, widest style range
- Grok 4: Best freshness (real-time web access), weaker polished long-form

## 🌐 Real-Time & Current Events

**Best: Grok 4**

Grok is built around live search and real-time tool use. If your task depends on information from the past few hours or days, Grok wins by default. Perplexity is also worth considering for structured research with citations.

## 🏢 Enterprise & Compliance

**Best: Microsoft Copilot / Claude**

Copilot holds FedRAMP authorization plus SOC 2 and HIPAA eligibility — the go-to for regulated industries. Claude is preferred for document-heavy reasoning workflows. GPT-5.5 has the largest enterprise integration ecosystem.
